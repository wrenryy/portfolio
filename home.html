A Little 'Bout Me

I've always been drawn to the overlap between concept and design. Creating out-of-this-world templates, logos and more in Adobe programs, (such as InDesign and Illustrator), and bringing it to life through HTML, CSS and Javascript.

From my early teenage days of building Howrse templates to trying to hack my own computer- my love of computer programming has blossomed it's roots. I got an education from Robertson College (2018-2019) and taken my skills up a notch in the real world. Since then I've mainly freelanced my skills wherever they are needed.

What once started as nothing but a hobby in my early teens with the infuence of freelancing: both concept art and graphic design have become a full-time passion and I've persued into more education at Visual College of Art & Design (VCAD, 2021-2022).

Freelance Work

Although I prefer having a steady enough job that I can rely on for mortgage payments, I am open to freelance for the right project/client.

I do comissions as well!.

Prefered Medium

Despite the fact that it completely depends on what a project/client needs, I prefer to do all traditonal art in Photoshop, Web Design in VS Code and Logos in Illustrator.

So I want an Art Comission....

My commission service is fairly simple. Once you send me a message asking for a comission I will likely respond within that 24 hour mark and send you a fairly simple forum explain what exactly you want. It does require a signature as neither of us want to end up in a difficult conflict of interest.

After that, the exchange is fairly simple. I sign the other half (both of us get a copy) and then I require half the payment up front.

I will send you the rough draft before I color. This insures that you get what you wanted. Once it's done I'll email you the digital copy and require the rest of the payment.
